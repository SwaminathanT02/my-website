Contractions Detection & Identification for Pregnancy Monitoring

A machine learning model utilising uterine muscle signals from 16 Ag/Cl electrodes placed on the abdomen, accurately distinguishing between labor contractions and Braxton-Hicks. This enables pregnant individuals to replace exhausting hospital visits in their 3rd trimester with at-home scans, enhancing convenience and reliable pregnancy monitoring.
